App Usage Guide

Getting Started

Download the app from the App Store or Google Play, and open it on your device. To access the full range of features, make sure you have an active internet connection.

Settings Screen

In the Settings screen, you can customize your experience:

  • Language Selection: Choose between English and Turkish.
  • Support Options: Show your support by making an in-app purchase.

Channel List Screen

This screen provides a list of available channels categorized as live, non-live, and favorite channels.

  • Adding M3U Links: You can add M3U links to view additional channels.
  • Channel Categorization: Browse channels by categories or access your favorite channels directly.
  • WebView & Video Player: Watch channels directly in the app’s video player or via WebView.

Channel Grid Screen

The Channel Grid screen displays channels in a grid format and allows you to view channels using WebView for a better experience.

Channel Slider

With the Channel Slider, you can easily navigate through available channels by swiping left or right.

Additional Features

  • Picture-in-Picture (PiP): Continue watching videos while you use other apps.
  • Save Favorites: Mark channels as favorites for quick access in the future.
